Interactive Web Applications

2023 | Frontend Development & UX Design

Across multiple projects, I designed and developed complete eCommerce web applications on customizable CMS platforms, combining front-end development and UX design. I created engaging user interfaces and layouts, including dynamic homepages, product listings, interactive menus, carousels, and playful micro-interactions that reinforced each brand’s identity. In addition to building reusable components and ensuring responsive, cross-device experiences, I managed ongoing content updates for seasonal campaigns, promotions, and product launches, keeping each site visually fresh and aligned with business goals. These projects showcase a balance of creative design, user experience, and technical execution.

SWEDEN UNLTD SHOP

Designed and developed the complete Sweden Unlimited merchandise store, bringing playful, brand-aligned interactions to life. Built a dynamic homepage with custom image banners, sliders, and animations, paired with engaging touches like a literal hamburger icon for the menu, a shopping cart that changes from sad when empty to happy when filled, and parallax overlay image scrolling for immersive visual depth. Created reusable components to maintain visual consistency across the site and delivered a fully responsive experience for seamless browsing on any device.

LemLem

As the lead developer, I designed and built a complete eCommerce site for Lemlem, an African-made clothing brand by translating their vibrant brand identity into an engaging online store. I created the account login/signup flow and customer dashboard to improve user engagement and retention, and built the majority of the product pages using HTML, CSS, and Liquid. The homepage features dynamic image banners, sliders, and animations to showcase products in an immersive way.

Live Site

Holly Yashi

Developed Holly Yashi’s website on a customizable CMS, including the architecture for the mega menu and designed tiny, custom icons that added a ‘cutesy’ charm to navigation in line with the jewelry brand’s aesthetic. I also incorperated interactive hover effects to enhance engagement, then built the product listings page, dynamic carousels, and a polished product page to deliver an immersive shopping experience.

Live Site

Veer Gear

Working with a team of three developers, I helped rebuild Veer’s luxury stroller website on a tight timeline, ensuring the final product matched the brand’s premium feel. I developed the majority of the site’s stylesheet, including buttons, reusable components, and form styling, and implemented intricate designs for the Switchback System page. Throughout the project, I collaborated closely with the team to ensure technical feasibility, maintain brand consistency, and create a highly interactive experience.
